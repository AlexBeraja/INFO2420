In the beginning

Rent A Pet began with a family that loved dogs. George Smith and his wife Carol owned two Irish Setters. These dogs were pets to the Smith family children and extended family members.

One day as George was walking his dogs in the park, he noticed how they attracted the attention of passing women. A young woman remarked that she would love to havea big dog like that to walk, but she had a small apartment and couldn't keep a large dog.

This brief encounter was the beginning of an idea. What if there were others who wanted the companionship of pets without the responsibility?

Advantages of the Business

  • Renters could have access to the pet at their leisure
  • Renters could pick the pet they wanted
  • Renters could exercise the pet
  • Renters could enjoy companionship
  • Renters would not pay upkeep or medical expenses
  • The owners could enjoy many pets

Disadvantages of the Business

  • The business must house and feed the pets
  • The business must provide medical care
  • The business must carry liability insurance
  • The owners are tied down by pet care

Where We Are Today

After weighing the advantages and disadvantages, the Smiths decided to move forward. In the fall of 1980, Rent A Pet officially opened.

The business began by renting dogs only. Since then, Rent A Pet has expanded to include cats, horses, pigs, and snakes.

What Makes Us Unique

Rent A Pet expanded from its original Provo location and now operates franchises in Logan, Bountiful, Salt Lake City, and St. George.

The company employs a trained veterinarian and an animal trainer who works with dogs and horses daily. Rent A Pet has never had an accident involving a rental pet.

Our customers include senior citizens, college students, families, and businesses. Pets are rented for companionship, exercise, parties, movies, and even shock value on special occasions.
